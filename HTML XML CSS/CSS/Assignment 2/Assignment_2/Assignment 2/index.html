WEB SITE STORY — The Smash Broadway Hit!

From the writers and directors that gave you the smash musicals, Crazy About You, and Barrels of Laughs comes their greatest work yet, Web Site Story — The Musical. This show takes you back in time to the Internet Revolution and weaves a story that blends comedy, drama, and song, to tell an enchanting tale of life in the new digital age. Set in Montreal in 2008, you'll experience a one-of-a-kind, breathtaking experience that will make you laugh and cry.
