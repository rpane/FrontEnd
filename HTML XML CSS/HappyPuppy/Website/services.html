DOGS HAVE SOCIAL LIFE

Dog Day Care

Our daycare facility was created to help dog owners with their daily dog parenting responsibilities. While in daycare, your dog will get exercise, socialization, snuggles, and will learn good canine manners. With the DoggyDay team by your side, your pup will come home happy and ready to snuggle.

HE'S ALLOWED SOME VACATION... TOO

Dog Boarding

When you leave town for a night, a weekend or for a trip, HappyPuppy offers you three types of dog boarding: in our condos designed specificially for his comfort, free - without a cage - under supervision, or at home. Get in touch with our team to know which boarding option is best for your dog.
